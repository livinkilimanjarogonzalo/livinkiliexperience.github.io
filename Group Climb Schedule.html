We offer Kilimanjaro group climbs for individuals who wish to climb with others to have extra company, meet new friends, and share their experiences during and after the trip. This is the most popular way to climb Mount Kilimanjaro.

The group consists of 5 to 10 climbers to ensure personalized attention and monitoring. Trekkers can easily share their experiences with others, making the climb even more memorable.

If you can’t find a suitable group date, don't worry! It's not necessary to follow the scheduled dates. You can contact us for your specific, suitable date and embark on the trip of a lifetime.
